# Young Cyber Knights Foundation (YCKF)

## Mission

To empower students, young professionals, institutions, and organizations through cybersecurity awareness, digital safety education, technology innovation, digital forensics training, and practical skill development to help build a safer digital future.

---

## Vision

To become a leading cybersecurity and digital innovation foundation in Africa dedicated to raising ethical cyber professionals, promoting digital safety, combating cybercrime, and advancing technology-driven solutions for society.

---

## Programs

* Cybersecurity Awareness Programs
* Digital Safety Education
* Digital Forensics & Cybercrime Investigation Training
* Full-Stack Software Development Internship Programs
* Cybersecurity Bootcamps & Workshops
* School & Institutional Cybersecurity Outreach
* Web & Mobile Application Development Projects
* Technology Innovation & Mentorship Programs
* Physical Security Awareness Education
* Youth Technology Empowerment Initiatives

---

## Internship Opportunities

YCKF provides internship and volunteer opportunities for students and young professionals interested in:

* Cybersecurity
* Digital Forensics
* Frontend Development
* Backend Development
* Mobile App Development
* UI/UX Design
* Graphic Design
* Content Creation
* Technical Support
* Project Management
* Cybersecurity Research

Interns gain practical experience through real-world projects, teamwork, mentorship, collaborative development, and cybersecurity awareness initiatives.
